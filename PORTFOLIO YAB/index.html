About Me

With 8 years of experience in 3D exhibition design, I help brands expose and reflect their image in front of the world. I use 3ds Max and V-Ray to create photorealistic visualizations that bring your booth concept to life before it's ever built.

My deliverables include a complete project folder: realistic 3D renders of your stand, plus a full technical file for builders — floor plans, wall plans, lighting layouts, hanging plans, furniture specs, dimensions, and material specifications.
